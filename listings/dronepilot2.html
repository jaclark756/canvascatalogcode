Description

Flying a drone can be intimidating. You may have asked yourself if you are going to crash or how high and how far you can fly or even if you are allowed to fly over people. If so, this course is for you. In this 3-hour course, participants learn the basic flight maneuvers and how to safely control the drone. The hands-on practice includes how to pick a safe area, how to complete a preflight inspection, how to take off, maneuver, and land while flying through an obstacle course.

Prerequisites:Drone Pilot 1: Safety & Compliance

Audience

  • Drone enthusiasts and hobbyists
  • Individuals seeking to fly to drones for commercial purposes.
